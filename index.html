How to create a Twitter Account

By MAG4 David

Twitter is one of the most-used Social Network Service(then just call it SNS,) and a lot of people use it.
We use Twitter for a lot of reasons. We can use it to upload pictures and discuss, and also we can use for people to notice something, and what you did today. But why do they use it?
==> There are a lot of good reasons, but it may be simple uploading, a lot of people in focus, "tweet"ing, and etc.

In this tutorial, you will learn the following : How to make a Twitter Account

Additional Info : Where does Twitter come from? Over here!



1. Go to the official Twitter Page.

Image 1-1 *Official Twitter Page

If you are at the official Twitter Webpage, it will show image 1-1 as main. Click Sign Up to register.


2. Type your name and email/phone number.

Image 2-1 *Basic Info

Enter your name (or nickname) correctly and your phone number. If you don't have a phone, use email instead. and vice versa.


3. Enter your password.

Image 3-1 *Password

Enter your password correctly. If you lose them, you won't be able to access to your Twitter account later.


4. Check Interests.

Image 4-1 *Interest

What are you interested at? Search the query.


5. Get recommended people to follow.

Image 5-1 *Recommend

This is the recommended list of people to follow.


6. Check for notifications.

Do you want notifications? (notify when you got tweets, messages, etc.)


7. Finish!

Image 7-1 *Finish

You are now finished! You can check your account by typing http://twitter.com/YourUserName. If there are capital letters 2x in row, from the second one, it will become lower case. To edit that, click the gray icon on the top right, and click Settings and Privacy, and rename the Username to your right Username.
